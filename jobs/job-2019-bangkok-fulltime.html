What We Are Looking For

Studio Lead & Video Producer

You would lead the set up and day-to-day management of a vodcast/podcast studio and produce content focused on startups, entrepreneurship, and innovation.

Ideally you are...

  • An experienced producer who has worked on independent film and commercial video projects.
  •  
  • A strategic thinker with business sense, able to incorporate the bigger picture into day-to-day decisions.
  • Detail oriented and able to troubleshoot and solve problems independently.
  • A great communicator who knows how to reach out for help when you need it and bring important issues to the attention of upper management.

Absolute must-have skills and experience:

  • Video editing (Final Cut Pro, Adobe Premier, iMovie)
  • Audio engineering

Bonus skills and experience:

  • Studio management & booking experience
  • Podcast production experience
  • Media presentation & interview skills
  • Marketing chops
