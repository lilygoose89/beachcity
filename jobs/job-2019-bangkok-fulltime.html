We're Hiring a Full Time Studio Lead & Video Producer

Are you great at social media? Interested in innovation? Startups? The future?

Want to join our social media team as a Social Media Manager or Admin?

We have part-time roles that could be just right for you.

BeachCity Media travel the globe in search of inspiring entrepreneurs and innovation hotspots. We interview the world's brightest founders, mentors and investors, sharing startup stories and incredible lessons in entrepreneurship. 

By sharing knowledge and insights straight from the people building the future, we can inspire and educate the next generation of aspiring entrepreneurs.

We're looking for digital storytellers, social media gurus and go-getters... people that are excited about life, technology and creating the future… great attitude needed!

This position can be Chiang Mai or Bangkok based or remote (preferred timezone UTC+7).

 

Open Positions

  • 1 Social Media Manager
  • 1 Social Media Admin

 

What We Are Looking For

Social Media Manager

Would suit a freelance digital marketer looking for a chance to develop their analytical chops and drive strategy.

Ideally you are a...

  • Social media pioneer who is constantly studying new technology and trends to discover the best ways to drive reach and engagement.
  • Facebook ads ninja, able to slice and dice audiences, split test, budget and optimize like a master.
  • Strategic thinker with business sense, able to incorporate the bigger picture into day-to-day decisions.
  • Snappy writer with impeccable attention to detail who can turn #hashtags and @callouts into copy that gets click-through.

 

Social Media Admin

Would suit a university student or recent grad eager to enter the world of social media marketing and learn more about startups.

All training is provided but prior experience is preferred.

Ideally you are...

  • Great at writing in English and have a sense of humor that comes across in copy, captions, and videos.
  • A social media native, highly engaged on Facebook, YouTube and Twitter.
  • Technically-minded and a fast-learner, able to pick up and master new software and tools quickly.
  • A team player, good at taking instruction and willing to ask questions to make sure you do the job right.
  • Thoughtful, attentive and detail oriented.
