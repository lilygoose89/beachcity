We're Hiring a Media Crew for Bangkok

Are you outgoing? Interested in innovation? Startups? Social media? The future?

Want to join our social media crew as a Presenter or Production Assistant?

We have part-time roles that could be just right for you.

BeachCity Media travel the globe in search of inspiring entrepreneurs and innovation hotspots.

When we rock up to the biggest tech events and startup hubs, we don't just hobnob with the suits, we're your boots on the ground, sharing stories and insights straight from the people building the future.

We interview the world's brightest founders, mentors and investors, sharing startup stories and incredible lessons in entrepreneurship

We're looking for storytellers, entrepreneurs and go-getters... young people that are excited about life, technology and creating the future… great attitude needed!

 

Open Positions

  • 1 Social Media Presenter (Native-English Fluency)
  • 2 Production Assistants

 

What we are looking for

Social Media Presenter

Would suit a tech or lifestyle blogger looking for opportunities to get in front of the camera, connect with a global audience, and network with Southeast Asia's startup leaders.

Ideally you are...

  • A confident speaker who is comfortable in front of a camera.
  • An engaging interviewer who is genuinely curious about innovation, entrepreneurship, startups, and technology.
  • Hungry and motivated to seek new opportunities, willing to go the extra-mile to make an incredible interview happen.
  • Native, or near-native, fluent in English and also highly proficient in Thai.
  • Eligible to work in Thailand.

 

Production Assistants

Would suit a university student or recent grad who would like to learn the secrets to producing engaging video content that goes viral on social media.

All training is provided. Prior experience is preferred but not required.

Ideally you are...

  • Good at communicating in English and also a native-speaker of Thai.
  • A team player, good at taking instruction and willing to ask questions to make sure you do the job right.
  • Thoughtful, attentive and detail oriented.
  • Eligible to work in Thailand.

 

When

Must be available for the following dates!

  1. Techsauce Global Summit | 19 - 20 June 2019
  2. Startup Nation Thailand | 25 - 27 July 2019

Ideally you will also be available for further opportunities in Bangkok and other exciting destinations in Asia including...

  • RISE Hong Kong |  8 - 11 July 2019
  • World Startup Expo India | 5 events from July to Dec 2019
  • Hong Kong FinTech Week | 4 - 8 November 2019

 

Compensation

Negotiable hourly rate depending on your experience. 

Expenses on location will be paid for including local travel and meals.
